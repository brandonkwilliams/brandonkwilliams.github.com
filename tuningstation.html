Guitar Case - Tuning Station Accessory

Spring/Summer 2012
All-State Industries Inc., Des Moines, Iowa

Mockup

Brainstorming

  • Design for a removable accessory that allows the user to prop the neck of the guitar up, out of the case in order to change strings or tune the instrument
  • When introduced to the idea, immediately created a mockup using my own guitar case to get a feel for how this might work
  • This mockup helped to generate more ideas for brainstorming the final design
  • Brainstormed different configurations that allowed optimum clearance and ease of use
  • Settled on this design with the neck rest serving a dual purpose as the locking mechanism on the box

Final Design

3D Printing

  • Created 3D engineering models using SolidWorks
  • Rendered the design to share with the client for final approvals
  • Final SolidWorks models were used to create 3D printings of the final design
  • The final product sitting within the recessed area of the foam padding
