Guitar Case

Spring/Summer 2012
All-State Industries Inc., Des Moines, Iowa

Brainstorming/Research

Initial Design

  • Researched existing products and client's provided images to determine taste and design ideas
  • Interviewed clients to determine wants and specifications
  • Created brainstorm sketches based on information received from clients
  • Used sketches to create SolidWorks models of three design ideas
  • Shared these models with the clients to receive feedback and determine design direction
  • Gathered design features from the client to be incorporated in the final design

Design for Prototyping

Final Prototype

  • Created engineering SolidWorks models for manufacturing processes: vacuum-formed plastic shells, CNC machined foam inserts, and rapid-prototyped Tuning Station accessory.

  • K2 Plastics Inc. manufactured and assembled a final prototype using the pieces that had been designed
  • It was rewarding to see the product through from an idea to a physical prototype

I learned so much from being the lead on a design project for an actual client. There were some minor mistakes made along the way due to miscommunication, but the learning that took place outweighed the errors.
