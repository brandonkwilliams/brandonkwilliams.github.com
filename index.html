Brandon Williams:
Engineer & Designer

Philosophy

I believe that truly good design cannot exist inside a bubble. It's not art, it's not engineering, it's not manufacturing, it's not marketing; it's everything involved in bringing an idea to life.

I believe that there aren't any skills that can't be learned, only those that have yet to be.

I believe in authenticity in design.

Process

Find Needs

Every project begins with a problem. Whether or not the stated problem is the right problem is often the most interesting challenge. It takes time and effort to truly understand the actual needs.

Associated Skills: User Research, Persona, Synthesis, Mood Board, Image Board

Ideate

The beginning of the fun for me. Starting to bring ideas out of my head and onto paper is an extremely rewarding task. I love to sketch and think through ideas visually and begin to develop form and function simultaneously.

Associated Skills: Sketching, SolidWorks, Photoview 360, Photoshop

Protoype

Another of my favorite parts of the process. To build physical representations of ideas requires a wide variety of skills and I am always eager to learn any skill that has potential value in prototyping.

Associated Skills: Foam Core Modeling, Wood-working, Arduino, Sewing, 3D Printing

Test

An idea is just that until it is validated. Testing ideas constantly with users to gain feedback is key if the end product hopes to be successful. Failing early and often is best.

Iterate

Test results should circle the process back to the beginning. Iteration involves redefining needs, more ideation, more prototyping, and more testing. This feedback loop allows me to evolve ideas until they are fully grown.

Manufacture

I've never been satisfied with only rough prototypes of potential products. To me, there is something so satisfying in the ability to bring the actual product to life. Although not all of my work gets manufactured, I take the approach that its not worth designing if it can't be made.

Associated Skills: Design for Manufacturing, Plastic Injection Molding, Plastic Vacuum-forming, Moldmaking, Wood-working, Welding
